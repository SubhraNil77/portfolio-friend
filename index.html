Welcome to My Dance Portfolio

I am Aritrika Mukherjee, a dedicated practitioner of Indian classical dance, specializing in the timeless and expressive art of Bharatnatyam. My journey into this intricate world began at a young age, nurtured by the teachings of esteemed gurus and a deep-rooted passion for this cultural heritage. With each performance, I strive to bring forth the profound stories, emotions, and rhythms that are intrinsic to our traditional dance forms. My portfolio is a reflection of my devotion to preserving and celebrating the elegance and depth of Indian classical dance, and I invite you to explore it to witness the grace, discipline, and artistry that define my work.

About Me

I have been performing and teaching Indian classical dance for over a decade. My training includes various styles such as Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Odissi. Dance is not just a form of art for me; it is a way to express my emotions and connect with my audience.
